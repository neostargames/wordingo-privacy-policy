# Privacy Policy for Wordingo

**Last updated:** April 22, 2026

Thank you for playing **Wordingo**. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our game.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We do **not** collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) directly.

However, we use third-party services that may collect certain information automatically, including:

* Device information (model, OS version)
* IP address
* Advertising ID (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
* App usage data (game interactions, session duration)
* Crash logs and performance data

---

## 2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

* Improve game performance and user experience
* Analyze gameplay behavior
* Provide and optimize advertisements
* Detect and prevent technical issues

---

## 3. Advertising

Wordingo uses **LevelPlay mediation** to serve ads. Through this mediation, the following ad networks may collect and process data:

* Unity Ads
* Google AdMob
* ironSource

These services may use cookies, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

---

## 4. Analytics

We use **Firebase Analytics** to understand how users interact with the game.

Firebase may collect:

* Device and usage data
* App events (e.g., level completion, session time)

---

Please review the privacy policies of our partners to understand how they handle your data:

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

However, data may be shared with third-party services listed above for:

* Advertising
* Analytics

## 7. Children's Privacy

Wordingo is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
